## WHAT TO DO (Step by Step)

### Step 1: Cancel current listing
- Contact your current agent/brokerage in writing
- Request MLS listing removal and written release
- Keep confirmation for your records

### Step 2: Sign up on Beycome ($99)
- Go to beycome.com
- Select Basic Package ($99)
- Your listing goes on the local MLS (which feeds to Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, Homes.com automatically)

### Step 3: Enter listing details
- Use the description from MLS_LISTING.md (in this folder)
- Upload your existing pro photos (from the current listing)
- Set price: $674,900
- Set buyer agent commission: 2.5%
- Fill in all property details per the MLS fields in MLS_LISTING.md

### Step 4: Hire a FL real estate attorney
- Budget: $800-$1,500
- They handle: purchase contract review, title search, closing docs, escrow
- Search: "real estate attorney Port Saint Lucie FL"
- Get one on retainer BEFORE you get offers

### Step 5: Get a yard sign
- Beycome Enhanced ($399) includes one, OR
- Order a custom FSBO sign from Amazon/Vistaprint (~$30-50)
- Include: "FOR SALE BY OWNER" + phone number + property website URL

### Step 6: Share the property website
- Post on Facebook Marketplace (free, massive reach)
- Post in local Port Saint Lucie / PSL / Treasure Coast Facebook groups
- Post on Craigslist (South Florida > real estate)
- Share link on your socials
