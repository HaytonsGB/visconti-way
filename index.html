About This Home

A Turnkey Lifestyle in PGA Village

Welcome to 10997 SW Visconti Way, a fully upgraded 3-bedroom plus den, 3-bathroom residence in the sought-after Verano at PGA Village. This 2,217 sq ft home delivers a turnkey lifestyle where modern comfort meets resort-style living.

Step inside to soaring high ceilings and an open-concept floor plan designed for both everyday living and effortless entertaining. The gourmet kitchen features a built-in oven, cooktop, and premium appliances. The living area is anchored by a striking fireplace, creating the perfect setting for cozy evenings or hosting guests.

The primary suite is a private retreat with a spa-inspired bathroom featuring dual vanities, a walk-in shower, and a generous walk-in closet. Two additional bedrooms and a flexible den provide space for guests, a home office, or whatever your lifestyle demands.
